About DroneDealz.in

DroneDealz.in is a student‑run side project built to make life easier for Indian makers, engineering students, and hobby pilots who are searching for the right motors, ESCs, frames and batteries for their first serious builds.

While working on college drone and defense projects it became clear that beginners waste a lot of time opening dozens of tabs, checking specs, reviews, and prices for every part. DroneDealz.in tries to solve this by collecting commonly used parts in one clean interface that loads quickly on mobile.

Parts shown here are picked for typical 3–5 inch quadcopters and DIY experiments, with a focus on availability in India, student‑friendly pricing, and compatibility. As more feedback comes from other students and labs, the plan is to expand into tuned part lists for FPV, cinematic builds, and training drones.

In the future, DroneDealz.in aims to add simple build guides, wiring diagrams, thrust‑to‑weight calculators, and ready‑made BOMs so that new builders can go from idea to flying prototype much faster without getting stuck on component choices.

This site is not an online shop. All “Buy” buttons simply redirect you to trusted marketplaces such as Amazon or Flipkart where you complete the purchase directly. DroneDealz.in does not handle payments or shipping; it only helps you compare and discover drone parts faster on top of those platforms.
